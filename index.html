A Simple Guide to Drum & Bass

Drum & Bass (DnB) is a fast-paced genre of electronic dance music that originated in the United Kingdom during the early 1990s. It is known for its rapid breakbeats, rolling basslines, and high-energy atmosphere. The genre draws inspiration from jungle, reggae, and rave culture. DJs and producers are constantly innovating, creating subgenres that range from dark and heavy to melodic and uplifting.
